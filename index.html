What I Do

Everything you need to launch and grow your business.

Brand Identity

Establishing the components related to a product or service. Some of these items are the name, logo, tone, tagline, typeface, and shape that create an appeal. Setting guidelines and consistency is essential to any brand.

Art Direction

Often referred to as communication design. It involves producing the content and images necessary to communicate your brand image with your target audience. Creating refined, impactful, and inspiring brand experiences that lead to positive outcomes.

Front End Development

Working on the client side and mixing the server side. Building both the visual and responsive components of a website. Experience building with but not limited to: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript and React.js.

Back End Development

Optimizing a project for speed and efficiency on the server-side and connecting with databases. Creating the logic with frameworks and databases such as: MySQL, MongoDB, Node.js and Express.js.
